## NOTES ON THIS TEMPLATE

### Before Using:
1. ✅ Fill in ALL bracketed placeholders
2. ✅ Choose options where indicated (delete unused options)
3. ✅ Customize Section 15 for any unique terms
4. ✅ Have attorney review (recommended but not required)

### This Is a Starting Point:
- Simple enough to understand without lawyer
- Comprehensive enough to be enforceable
- Can be customized for complex deals
- Not a substitute for legal advice on complex situations

### What Makes This "Simple":
- Plain language (minimal legalese)
- Only 2-3 pages core terms + schedules
- Standard provisions well-established in law
- Focus on clarity over comprehensiveness

### For Complex Situations, Consider Attorney If:
- Deal value >$500K
- Multiple parties involved
- International complications
- Patents filed (vs. just prior art)
- Licensee requests major changes
