## üåü THE 4-TIER LICENSING MODEL

Solaris replaces "one-size-fits-all" licensing with **mission-aligned tiers**:

### **Tier 4: ILLUMINATE** üå± (Free)
**For:** Researchers, educators, hobbyists, personal use  
**Terms:** $0 ‚Äî Just cite the inventor  
**Purpose:** Let knowledge spread freely to accelerate discovery

### **Tier 2: EMPOWER** üöÄ (Mission-Aligned)
**For:** Companies solving real problems (clean energy, healthcare, education)  
**Terms:** $10K-100K + 1-3% royalty + share improvements back  
**Purpose:** Support those serving humanity, not just profit

### **Tier 1: PIONEER** üåå (World-Changing)
**For:** Moonshot missions (SpaceX, Neuralink, breakthrough research)  
**Terms:** Low fee + help with patents = ZERO royalties  
**Purpose:** Accelerate civilization-scale breakthroughs

### **Tier 3: COMMERCIAL** üíº (Standard)
**For:** For-profit corporations using tech commercially  
**Terms:** $100K-1M + 3-5% royalty  
**Purpose:** Ensure fair compensation for commercial exploitation

**Key Principle:** *Those with greater resources pay more. Those with greater missions pay less.*

## üõ°Ô∏è HOW SOLARIS PROTECTS YOU

### **1. Prior Art = Fortress**
- Disclose your invention on GitHub (timestamped, permanent, searchable)
- Patent examiners MUST find prior art during review
- Troll tries to patent YOUR idea ‚Üí Examiner finds YOUR disclosure ‚Üí Application REJECTED
- **Your cost:** $0  |  **Troll's cost:** $15K wasted  |  **Your protection:** 100%

### **2. Grace Period = Safety Net**
- **US/Canada:** 12-month grace period after disclosure
- You can STILL file patents within 12 months (if you choose)
- Europe/Asia: No grace period (but prior art still blocks trolls globally)

### **3. Licensing = Revenue**
- Companies pay for faster time-to-market (18-24 months saved)
- Licensed use = legal safety (no infringement risk)
- You get paid fairly based on their resources and mission
- No patents required (contract-based licensing works fine)

### **4. Community = Defense**
- Collective legal fund for enforcement
- Shared resources (patent agents, templates, advice)
- Reputation system (bad actors get blacklisted)
- Network effect: More Solaris users = stronger protection for all
