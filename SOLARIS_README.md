## üõ°Ô∏è HOW PROTECTION WORKS

### The Prior Art Shield

**Step 1:** You disclose your invention publicly (GitHub, blog, paper)  
**Step 2:** Disclosure is timestamped and permanent  
**Step 3:** Patent offices worldwide now know about it  
**Step 4:** Anyone trying to patent YOUR idea gets rejected  

**Result:** Trolls can't touch you. Ever.

### The Grace Period Window

**In US/Canada:** You have 12 months after disclosure to file patents  
**Everywhere else:** Your prior art still blocks trolls (just can't get patents yourself in some countries)

**Strategic choice:**
- Want global patents? ‚Üí File provisional BEFORE disclosure
- Don't care about patents? ‚Üí Disclose immediately, save $60K-130K

### The Licensing Shield

**Tier 4 (free) users MUST:**
- Cite you properly
- Use only for non-commercial purposes
- Not redistribute as their own

**Commercial users MUST:**
- License through Tiers 1-3
- Pay agreed fees and royalties
- Follow ethical guidelines
- Share improvements (Tiers 1-2)

**Violators face:**
- Cease and desist letters
- License termination
- Community blacklisting
- Legal action (funded by community defense fund)
